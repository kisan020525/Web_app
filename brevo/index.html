What Brevo Does

  • Email marketing: Drag‑and‑drop editor, templates, A/B testing, segmentation, and automation workflows.
  • Multi‑channel: SMS, WhatsApp campaigns, push notifications, live chat, phone, meeting booking, and web push.
  • CRM & sales: Built‑in CRM, deal pipelines, tasks, meeting scheduling, and customer data platform.
  • APIs: REST APIs for email/SMS/WhatsApp, contacts, companies, and transactional messages.

Company Overview

Background: Previously known as Sendinblue; rebranded to Brevo.

Founded: 2012, with global offices and European roots.

Scale: Hundreds of thousands of customers and over a thousand employees, focused on SMBs.

Main Product Modules

Marketing Platform

Email/SMS/WhatsApp campaigns, landing pages, forms, and analytics.

Conversations Platform

Website chat widget, shared inbox, chatbots, and phone tools.

Sales Platform

CRM, pipeline management, tasks, and email tracking for sales teams.

Messaging API

High‑deliverability infrastructure for transactional email, SMS, and WhatsApp.
