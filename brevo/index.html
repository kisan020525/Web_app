AngyOne Verdict
The "Smart Money" Choice

Brevo is the best tool for SMBs who have a large contact list but send infrequent emails. It is roughly 50% cheaper than Mailchimp because they don't charge you for storing contacts.

Overall Rating: 9.0/10
✅ Use Brevo If...
  • You have a large list: You can store unlimited contacts for free. You only pay when you hit "Send".
  • You want "Everything" in one place: It handles Emails, SMS, WhatsApp, and basic CRM deals in one tab.
  • You are budget-conscious: The "Starter" plan ($9) removes the daily sending limit.
  • You need Transactional Emails: Their API for password resets/order confirmations is top-tier.
❌ Avoid Brevo If...
  • You need world-class Analytics: Their reporting is functional but not deep like HubSpot.
  • You want the "Easiest" UI: The editor is good, but Mailchimp's design is slightly more intuitive for beginners.
  • You are a large Enterprise: If you need complex permissions and custom SLA, Salesforce/HubSpot is better.

Pricing Reality Check

Plan Cost The Catch
Free $0 300 emails/day limit. Good for testing, bad for launching.
Starter ~$9/mo Best value. Removes daily limit. Includes basic reporting.
Business ~$18/mo Adds Automation, Facebook Ads, and Landing Pages.

Hidden Cost Warning: "Remove Brevo Logo" is an paid add-on in the Starter plan (usually ~$10 extra).
