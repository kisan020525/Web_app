Brevo vs Mailchimp: 2025 Showdown

Why thousands of businesses are switching from Mailchimp to Brevo this year.

If you have been using Mailchimp for years, you are probably "fine" with it. But you are likely also paying for users who haven't opened your emails in months.

The industry has shifted. Payment models have changed.

At a Glance

Feature Brevo (Winner) Mailchimp
Free Plan Limit Unlimited Contacts 500 Contacts
Pricing Model Pay for EMAILS SENT Pay for LIST SIZE

SMS Marketing Included Extra Cost

1. The Contact Tax

This is the #1 reason to switch.

Mailchimp charges you based on how many people are on your list. If you have 10,000 subscribers but only email them once a month, you pay a fortune.

Brevo charges you based on how many emails you send. You can have 1,000,000 contacts for free. You only pay when you actually market to them.

"We saved $180/month just by switching to Brevo because we have a large, inactive list."

2. Automation for Everyone

Mailchimp restricts advanced automation (like "If user clicks X, wait 2 days, then send Y") to higher paid tiers.

Brevo gives you the full Automation Workflow builder on the Free Plan (up to 2,000 contacts). This is a game changer for small businesses establishing their first funnel.

The Verdict

If you are a massive enterprise, Mailchimp's integrations might be necessary. But for 99% of SMBs, Creators, and Startups, Brevo is mathematically the better choice.
