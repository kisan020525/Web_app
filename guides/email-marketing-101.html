What is Email Marketing? The Complete Guide

Learn the 5 steps, the 80/20 rule, and exactly how to start sending emails that make money.

What is Email Marketing?

Email marketing is the act of sending a commercial message to a group of people using email. In its broadest sense, every email sent to a potential or current customer could be considered email marketing.

But why is it good? ROI (Return on Investment). For every $1 spent on email marketing, the average return is $42. It is the most profitable channel available.

What are the 5 Steps of Email Marketing?

If you are just starting, follow this proven framework:

  1. Strategy: Define your audience and goals.
  2. List Building: Get people to sign up (legally).
  3. Automation: Set up "Welcome" emails that send automatically.
  4. Campaigns: Send regular newsletters or offers.
  5. Analysis: Check open rates and improve.

What is the 80/20 Rule in Email Marketing?

The 80/20 rule suggests that 80% of your emails should be valuable content (educational, entertaining, helpful), and only 20% should be sales pitches.

If you sell in every email, people will unsubscribe. Build trust first, then sell.

What are the 4 P's?

  • Product: What are you selling?
  • Price: Is it affordable for your list?
  • Place: Where do they read it? (Mobile vs Desktop).
  • Promotion: How do you present the offer?
