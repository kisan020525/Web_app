However, their design has historically been a computationally intensive and iterative process.

The core innovation lies in Meta-GPT's ability to learn and understand the intricate relationships between the geometric features of a metasurface and its resulting optical properties. This understanding allows the AI to predict the behavior of novel metasurface designs with remarkable accuracy, drastically cutting down the time and resources required for development.
